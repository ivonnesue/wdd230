About Us

Bountiful Foods started 5 years ago when Jane and Joe Wells began their journey for a healthier lifestyle. Jane found that making fruit juices was a key routine that helped them reach their health goals. "We believe in the power that nature has to make you a healthier and stronger person". We've have always wanted to find the highest quality local produce, but it was only available at larger grocery stores farther from neighborhoods and small businesses. That is how Bountiful Foods was born. We discovered a need for local availability of fresh organic produce. We started small by partnering with local farmers and now we are growing pretty fast! We strive to make produce shopping a convenient experience were best best products are easy to find. Please come visit us at our store and food truck locations.
