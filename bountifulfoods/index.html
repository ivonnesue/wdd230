San Pedro Coastal Area

San Pedro Farmer's Market

San Pedro is a neighborhood within the City of Los Angeles, California, United States. Formerly a separate city, it consolidated with Los Angeles in 1909. The Port of Los Angeles, a major international seaport, is partially located within San Pedro. The district has grown from being dominated by the fishing industry, to a working-class community within the city of Los Angeles, to a rapidly gentrifying community.
Visit our Fruit Truck at San Pedro's Farmers Market, 396 W 6th St, San Pedro, CA 90731

San Clemente Coastal Area

San Clemente Farmer's Market

San Clemente is a city in Orange County, California. It's known for San Onofre and San Clemente state beaches, with their surf breaks and sandstone bluffs. Running along the coast, the Beach Trail offers sea views and green parks. Near T-Street Beach, long San Clemente Pier stretches out into the Pacific Ocean. Casa Romantica Cultural Center and Gardens embodies the city's original Spanish-style architecture.
Visit our Fruit Truck at San Clemente Farmers Market, 243 Avenida del Mar, San Clemente, CA, 92672

Coronado Beach

Coronado Beach Certified Farmer's Market

Coronado (Spanish for "Crowned") is a resort city located in San Diego County, California, United States, across the San Diego Bay from downtown San Diego.It was founded in the 1880s and incorporated in 1890. Swimming, sunbathing, surfing & other outdoor recreation draws crowds to this scenic, urban beach.
Visit our Fruit Truck at Coronado Certified Farmers Market, 1st St & B Ave, Coronado, CA 92118
