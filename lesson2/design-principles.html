Repetition

Behance

Behance is a portfolio platform where creative professionals show their skills. In heir home page, they display projects in boxes, which repeat in a grid vertically or horizontally, depending on the view size. In the image above, it shows how the boxes repeat in a one colum grid with multiple rows.

Contrast

Apple

The apple website is a great example of contrast. In the home page they display a hero image with black as background color, white for heading. The black background also highlights the colors of the products

Rule of Thirds

Navy Federal

I chose the Navy Federal home page as an example of the rule of thirds. The rule of thirds is a way of layouting where objects are placed in a grid equally into thirds, horizontally or vertically. In the second section we see three boxes with equal width and the bottom below this boxes also covers the entire width of the grid in the small view.
