Site Planning

Target Audience

Established businesses who desire to form and strenghten business relationship and with the community. New entrepreneurs who need support to grow their business, increase their networking efforts, and gain visibility in the community

Personas

John and Anna represent the users of our website

John

Description

  • Demographics: John represents users between 30 - 50 years old
  • Income: Users like John have steading income through their businesses
  • Experience: Has more than 10 years of experience in their field
  • Goals: Wants to gain credibility and recognition in the community, strenghtens or form new business relationships, create partnertships, wants to be easily found online

Scenarios

1. How do I find information about the local Chamber of Commerce's activities and events?
2. How do I connect with other local companies whom I might do business with?
3. If not a member yet, What are the benefits of joining the Chamber membership?
4. How do I become a member?, or how do I upgrade my membership?
5. How do I contact the Chamber's office?

Anna

Description

  • Demographics: Anna represents new entrepreneurs of different ages, usually betweeb 25 and 50
  • Income: minimun to no income through their business
  • Experience: Has between 1 and 5 years of experience in as entrepreneur
  • Goals: Wants to learn how to grow her business, reach new customers or gain visibility, find professional individuals or companies to form a business relationship, wants to be part of the Chamber membership

Scenarios

1. How do I find information about the Chamber's Business support Programs, events, and activities?
2. How do I find companies and professionals to expand my networking contacts?
3. How do I contact the Chamber's Office?
4. How do I know the benefits of joining the Chamber?
